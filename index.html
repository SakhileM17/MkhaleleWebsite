Helman Mkhalele School Sports League.

HMSSL is a schools sports leagues founded by Coach Helman Mkhalele in his home town Newcastle in Kwazulu Natal. The League was contested within the Amajuba District Municipality in KwaZulu Natal across two sporting codes, Football and Netball.
