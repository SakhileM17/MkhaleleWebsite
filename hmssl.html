Helman Mkhalele Schools Sports League.


HMSSL is one of may community projects started by Helman Mkhalele to make a difference in the community, the schools sports leagues was implemented in his home town Newcastle in Kwazulu Natal and he aspires to grow the league nationally.

The League was contested within the Amajuba District Municipality in KwaZulu Natal across two sporting codes, Football and Netball from junior leavel in primary school and all the way up to senior level in high schools.
